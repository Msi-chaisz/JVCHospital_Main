Medicine Stocks

Paracetamol

Type: Analgesic (pain reliever)

Use: Reduces pain and fever

Common Brands: Biogesic, Tylenol

Dosage Form: Tablet, syrup, drops


Side Effects: Rare (possible liver damage if overdosed)

Precaution: Avoid alcohol while taking

Prescription: Over-the-counter

Type: NSAID

Use: Pain, inflammation, fever

Dosage Form: Tablet, capsule, syrup

Precaution: Take with food

Prescription: OTC (L-dose), prescription (H-dose)
